KENNEDY SPACE CENTER(UNITED STATES)

Located at Cape Canveral, Florida, Kennedy Space Center is perhaps the world's best-known spaceport. The Center was host to key milestones in the Space Race of the 1950s and 1960s, including:

  • The launch of the first American satellite, Explorer 1, in 1958
  • The first suborbital and orbital manned American flights in 1961 and 1962 respectively
  • The launch of Apollo 11 in 1969
